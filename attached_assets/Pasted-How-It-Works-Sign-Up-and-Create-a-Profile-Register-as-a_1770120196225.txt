How It Works:
Sign Up and Create a Profile: Register as a service provider and create a profile highlighting your skills and experience.
Bid on Tasks: Browse available tasks in your area and submit your bid. Include any relevant experience to increase your chances of being selected.
Complete the Task: Once selected, complete the task to the best of your ability. Good communication and professionalism are key to receiving positive reviews.
Get Paid: After completing a task, the client will pay you directly, either in cash or through mobile money services like Mpesa.
FAQs
For Clients:
•	How do I post a task?
It’s easy! Simply create an account, select the category of your task, and provide a description along with any necessary details. Service providers in your area will then place bids, and you can choose the one that best fits your needs.
•	How do I know my service provider is trustworthy?
All service providers on Errander undergo a thorough vetting process, including identity verification and, where applicable, professional certification. Additionally, you can review ratings and feedback from previous clients before making your selection.
•	What if I’m not satisfied with the service?
If you’re unhappy with the service provided, you can contact our support team. We take client satisfaction seriously and will work to resolve any issues.
For Service Providers:
•	What kind of tasks can I bid on?
You can bid on tasks that fall within the specific category you selected during the onboarding process. This ensures that you’re only offered tasks that match your expertise and skills, allowing you to focus on what you do best.
•	How do I get paid?
After completing a task, the client will pay you directly, either in cash or through mobile money services like Mpesa. Make sure to confirm the payment method with the client before starting the task to ensure a smooth transaction. Keep in mind that maintaining good communication and professionalism can help you secure timely payments and positive reviews.
•	What if I need to cancel a task?
If you need to cancel after accepting a task, please notify the client as soon as possible. Frequent cancellations may affect your rating and standing within the Errander community.
